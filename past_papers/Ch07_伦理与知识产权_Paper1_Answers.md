## 2024 Oct/Nov (9618/12)

### Question 5

(a) Explain the reasons why the computer programmer needs to act ethically towards colleagues and the public. [4 marks]

**Colleagues:**
✓ The programmer should respect the intellectual property / work of colleagues and not claim credit for others' work
✓ The programmer should not access or modify colleagues' code/data without permission
✓ The programmer should report bugs/vulnerabilities honestly rather than hiding them
✓ The programmer should share knowledge and collaborate fairly, contributing to a trustworthy working environment

> 提示：对同事 - 尊重知识产权、不擅自访问他人代码、诚实报告错误、公平合作

**The public:**
✓ The programmer should ensure software is safe and reliable / thoroughly tested before release, to avoid harm to users
✓ The programmer should protect user data / privacy and not misuse personal information collected by the software
✓ The programmer should be honest about the capabilities and limitations of the software
✓ The programmer should consider the social impact of the software and avoid creating software that could be used for harmful purposes

> 提示：对公众 - 确保软件安全可靠、保护用户隐私、诚实说明软件功能、考虑社会影响

*2 marks for colleagues + 2 marks for the public = 4 marks*

---

(b)(i) Identify one suitable type of software licence and describe the reasons for your choice. [3 marks]

- Type of software licence: ✓ **Open source licence** (1 mark)

- Reasons for choice:
✓ Open source allows the business to view and modify the source code, which matches the requirement that "the source code can be modified by the business"
✓ Open source licences often allow the developer to charge for support/maintenance/security updates, which matches the requirement that "the business pays the programmer for maintenance and security updates"

> 提示：开源许可证 - 允许查看和修改源代码（符合题目要求），开发者可收取维护和安全更新费用

*1 mark for identification + 2 marks for reasons = 3 marks*

---

(b)(ii) Explain the reasons why the programmer should copyright their program. [2 marks]

✓ Copyright gives the programmer legal ownership / intellectual property rights over the program, preventing others from copying, distributing or selling it without permission
✓ Copyright allows the programmer to take legal action against anyone who infringes their rights / uses the program without authorisation, protecting their income/livelihood

> 提示：版权保护 - 赋予法律所有权防止他人未经许可复制/分发；可对侵权者采取法律行动保护收入

## 2022 Oct/Nov (9618/12)

### Question 9
Describe the social impact of using facial recognition software to identify individuals in an airport. [2 marks]

**Positive impacts:**
✓ Facial recognition can improve security by quickly identifying known criminals/terrorists, making airports safer for the public
✓ It can speed up the check-in/boarding process, reducing waiting times for passengers

**Negative impacts:**
✓ It raises privacy concerns as individuals are being monitored/tracked without their explicit consent
✓ The technology may produce false positives, leading to innocent people being wrongly identified and detained
✓ There are concerns about bias in the AI algorithms that may lead to discrimination against certain ethnic groups

> 提示：面部识别社会影响 - 正面：提高安全性、加快通关；负面：隐私侵犯、误识别导致冤枉、算法偏见可能歧视特定群体

*Must include at least one positive AND one negative impact for full marks. 2 marks.*
